About us

Broomboom is yours and what is yours will always find you!

Broomboom is one of the leading bikes taxi cab aggregator in West Bengal. With lots of hard work and perseverance we have made a place for ourselves in the Market.
The idea popped out during pandemic as the founders of Broomboom: Swarnava Roy, Gourab Das & Prity Prasad, saw that during the pandemic, people had to face many problems due to unavailability of transport services. Even a single ambulance was unavailable at the hour of need creating a gap between demand and suppy. Since middle-class citizens were unable to afford the transport at the hour of need, many people not only lost their job but also lost their lives due to unavailability of basic transport facilities.
Looking at such chaos that was going on, the founders of Broomboom started analysing and studying the market. After a lot of research and analysis, it got concluded that people want affordable and easily available transport services. This encouraged the founders of the Broomboom to come and provide such services to the middle-class people that not only give them affordable, quick and safe ride service but also be available at any time the users want. Even if the user wants, they can schedule or book a ride in advance and Broomboom reaches to pick them on time.
Just like a well-wisher, Broomboom stood beside the public during the pandemic and is still helping them to reach their destination on time. As we all know good things take time to evolve and same happened with Broomboom, the end of the pandemic marked a new beginning of Broomboom.
